The word set of the HC text corpus was expected to mainly correspond to the standard English.
Trivial English words and expressions were expected to be on the top by the frequency.
Typos, conscious mutilations and word-building are usual in Internet language.
Written expressive language of the news might differ from that of blogs or Twitter, the possible differences would not be studied or considered at that moment.
Internet jargon and dialects like smiley's and emojies should become the subject of a studies, other than this one.
Non-english words will be ignored, having the final goal of prediction in mind.
